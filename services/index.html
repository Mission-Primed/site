FAQ

Questions people ask before they trust someone with their systems.

Do you do one-time fixes?

Yes — but we’ll still document what we touch and recommend a baseline if we find serious gaps. One-time work without standards turns into recurring fires.

Can you work with our current vendors?

Yes. We’ll inventory what you have, clean up access, and create a vendor/contact list so nothing is trapped in one person’s inbox.

Do you support Mac and Windows?

Yes. The goal is consistent policy, patched devices, strong identity controls, and predictable support — regardless of OS.

How fast is response?

It depends on the plan and hours. We set expectations in writing and prioritize outages/security incidents. No guessing games.

Are you “MSP style” or “security-first”?

Security-first — but practical. We don’t break productivity. We lock down what matters, and document every decision.

What do you need from us to start?

A short intake call, current admin contacts, and approval to inventory devices/accounts. We’ll do the heavy lift.

Ready to stabilize your IT?

Tell us your headcount, device count, and what’s been breaking. We’ll recommend the cleanest path forward.
