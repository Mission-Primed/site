What we support

Aligned deliverables for government and regulated environments.

Control-aligned implementation

  • Identity hardening, privileged access model, MFA enforcement.
  • Patch workflows, baseline configuration, change control.
  • Logging requirements and retention planning.

Evidence packs

  • System diagrams, inventories, SOPs, and runbooks.
  • Evidence capture: configuration screenshots, exports, and logs.

POA&M / remediation support

  • Prioritization, timelines, and ownership mapping.

Policy & procedures

Sustainment

  • Ticket-driven evidence trails.
